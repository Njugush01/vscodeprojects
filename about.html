Who Are We?

Over a decade ago, we started a store to sell snowboards online. None of the ecommerce solutions at the time gave us the control we needed to be successful—so we built our own. Today, businesses of all sizes use Cara, whether they’re selling online, in retail stores, or on-the-go.Cara has grown from 5 people in a coffee shop to over 10,000 across the globe. With millions of businesses powered by Cara, we care deeply about the work we do. We’re constant learners who thrive on change and seek to have an impact in everything we do.
